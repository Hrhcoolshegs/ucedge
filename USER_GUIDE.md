### Key Capabilities
- **Customer Base**: 850,000+ customers
- **Transaction Volume**: 5,000,000+ transactions
- **Real-time Analytics**: Live dashboards and metrics

## Data & Metrics

### Understanding the Numbers

#### **Customer Lifecycle Stages**
1. **New**: Recently acquired customers (< 30 days)
2. **Active**: Engaged customers with recent transactions
3. **At-Risk**: Declining engagement, potential churn
4. **Churned**: No activity for 90+ days
5. **Reactivated**: Previously churned, now active again

#### **Key Performance Indicators**

**Customer Metrics**:
- Total Customers: 850,000+ (actual: 2,000 Ã— 425 display multiplier)
- Active Rate: % of customers with recent activity
- Churn Rate: % of customers lost per period
- Reactivation Rate: % of churned customers won back

**Revenue Metrics**:
- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
- Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)
- Revenue Growth Rate

**Engagement Metrics**:
- Login Frequency
- Feature Adoption
- Transaction Frequency
- Support Interactions

**Sentiment Metrics**:
